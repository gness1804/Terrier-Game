The Five Scottish Terrier Breeds

Click each breed name to see the AKC Breed Standard!

Scottish Terrier

The Scottish Terrier, or "Scottie", is a loyal and spunky dog. Scotties can be distinguished from other terriers of this group by the distinctive style of hair on the snout and around the eyes. Their coats can range from the traditional black to the lighter "wheaten" color. Wikipedia page.

West Highland White Terrier

West Highland White Terriers, more commonly known as "Westies", are a loyal and affectionate dog that is characterized by a white coat. Like many other terriers, it was bred to ferret out rodents, and its "digging" instinct is accordingly strong. It is a loyal and caring dog. Wikipedia page.

Cairn Terrier

The Cairn Terrier is a hardy and self-confident dog closely related to the Westie. For more on the Cairn, see my Cairn Terrier page and the Wikipedia page on the breed.

Skye Terrier

The Skye Terrier is a dog known for is "shaggy" appearance and was bred to hunt. This breed is among the oldest of Scotland's terriers. Wikipedia page.

Dandie Dinmont Terrier

The Dandie Dinmont Terrier is known for its distinctive hair pattern on its head. It is a bit rarer than some other dogs featured on this page, and it can come in a variety of coat colors. Wikipedia page.
